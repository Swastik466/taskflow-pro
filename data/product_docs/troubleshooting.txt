TASKFLOW PRO — TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
======================================

## Login & Authentication Issues

**Problem: Cannot log in — "Invalid email or password" error.**
Solution:
1. Check that Caps Lock is off.
2. Try resetting your password at app.taskflowpro.com/forgot-password.
3. Check your spam/junk folder for the reset email.
4. If using SSO (Business plan), contact your IT administrator — SSO issues are managed by your organisation.

**Problem: Verification email not received.**
Solution:
1. Check spam/junk folder.
2. Wait up to 5 minutes, then request a new verification email from the login page.
3. Ensure your email server is not blocking app.taskflowpro.com. Add it to your safe senders list.

**Problem: Account locked after too many failed attempts.**
Solution: Accounts are locked after 10 consecutive failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes for the lockout to expire, or contact support@taskflowpro.com to unlock immediately.

## Task & Project Issues

**Problem: Cannot create more projects (Free plan).**
Solution: The Free plan supports a maximum of 3 projects. To create more, upgrade to Pro at app.taskflowpro.com/billing.

**Problem: Task not appearing after creation.**
Solution:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R).
2. Check that you are viewing the correct project and status column.
3. If the task was assigned to someone else, it may be filtered out. Clear all active filters.

**Problem: File attachment fails to upload.**
Solution:
1. Check file size: Free plan max is 100 MB, Pro is 500 MB.
2. Ensure file format is supported (PDF, PNG, JPG, DOCX, XLSX, ZIP).
3. Check your internet connection stability.
4. Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).

**Problem: Gantt chart is not visible.**
Solution: Gantt Chart is only available on Pro and Business plans. Upgrade your plan to access this feature.

---

## Notifications Issues

**Problem: Not receiving email notifications.**
Solution:
1. Go to Profile > Notifications and confirm email notifications are enabled.
2. Add noreply@taskflowpro.com to your safe senders list.
3. Check spam/junk folder.
4. If issues persist, contact support.

**Problem: Receiving too many notifications.**
Solution: Go to Profile > Notifications and customise which events trigger notifications. You can disable notifications for comments, status changes, or specific projects individually.

---

## Performance Issues

**Problem: App is slow or pages take long to load.**
Solution:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Try an incognito/private window.
3. Disable browser extensions one by one to identify conflicts.
4. Ensure your browser is up to date (latest Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari supported).
5. Check our status page at status.taskflowpro.com for known outages.

**Problem: Mobile app crashes on startup.**
Solution:
1. Force-close and restart the app.
2. Ensure you have the latest version installed (check your app store).
3. Restart your device.
4. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app.

---

## Data & Sync Issues

**Problem: Changes made on mobile are not showing on web.**
Solution:
1. Pull to refresh on the mobile app.
2. Wait up to 60 seconds — sync may be delayed on slow connections.
3. Force-close the mobile app and reopen it.
4. Log out and log back in to force a full sync.

---

## Known Error Codes
- ERR-401: Session expired. Log out and log back in.
- ERR-403: You do not have permission to perform this action. Contact your workspace admin.
- ERR-429: Too many requests. Wait 60 seconds and try again.
- ERR-500: Internal server error. Check status.taskflowpro.com. If no outage is listed, contact support.
