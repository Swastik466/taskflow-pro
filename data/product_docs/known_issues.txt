TASKFLOW PRO — KNOWN ISSUES & SERVICE STATUS
=============================================

Last updated: May 2026

## Currently Active Known Issues

### KI-2024-001: Gantt Chart Slow to Load for Projects with 500+ Tasks
Status: In Progress
Affected Plans: Pro, Business
Description: Projects containing more than 500 tasks may experience 5–15 second delays when opening the Gantt Chart view. The chart eventually loads correctly.
Workaround: Filter tasks by date range or assignee before opening the Gantt view to reduce the number of tasks rendered.
Expected Fix: Patch release scheduled for June 2026.

### KI-2024-002: Slack Integration Notifications Delayed
Status: Monitoring
Affected Plans: Pro, Business
Description: Some users report Slack notifications arriving 5–10 minutes late. This is caused by Slack API rate limits on our shared integration endpoint.
Workaround: Use in-app or email notifications as a primary notification channel.
Expected Fix: Architecture change to per-workspace Slack app in Q3 2026.

### KI-2024-003: CSV Export Truncates Task Descriptions Over 1,000 Characters
Status: Confirmed
Affected Plans: All plans
Description: When exporting tasks to CSV, descriptions longer than 1,000 characters are truncated without warning.
Workaround: Export in JSON format (Settings > Data Export > Export as JSON) to retain full descriptions.
Expected Fix: Next major release.

## Recently Resolved Issues

### KI-2024-004: Mobile App Crash on iOS 17.4 (RESOLVED)
Resolved: April 2026
Description: The TaskFlow Pro iOS app crashed on launch for users running iOS 17.4 after an Apple system update.
Fix Applied: App Store update v4.12.1 resolved the crash. Ensure you are on v4.12.1 or later.

### KI-2024-005: Email Notifications Sent Twice for Comment Mentions (RESOLVED)
Resolved: March 2026
Description: Users received duplicate email notifications when mentioned in task comments.
Fix Applied: Server-side deduplication logic deployed on 15 March 2026.

## Reporting a New Bug
If you encounter an issue not listed here:
1. Click the "?" icon in the app and select "Report a Bug".
2. Describe the issue, your browser/OS version, and steps to reproduce.
3. Attach screenshots if possible.
4. You will receive a ticket confirmation by email. Our engineering team reviews all bug reports within 2 business days.
